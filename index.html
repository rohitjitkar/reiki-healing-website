Understanding

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that supports your body's natural ability to restore balance and well-being. It works with the subtle energy that flows through all living beings, helping to release blockages and promote deep relaxation.

Through distance Reiki, healing energy is transmitted across time and space, guided by intention and compassion. It is a complementary wellness practice that pairs beautifully with medical care to support your overall health journey.

Reiki is a complementary wellness practice and is not a substitute for medical care. Always consult your healthcare provider for any medical concerns.
